# Which DnD Class Fits Your Dog's Breed?

Some things are just true. Golden Retrievers are Paladins. Border Collies are Wizards. Huskies are Barbarians. This is not up for debate.

Here's a comprehensive breakdown of dog breeds by DnD class. Use this as a starting point — but remember, your dog's actual personality always wins over breed stereotypes.

## Paladin — Noble, Loyal, Devoted

The Paladin is defined by loyalty, protection, and calm confidence. These breeds wear it naturally.

- **Golden Retriever** — The platonic ideal of a Paladin. Devoted, gentle, incapable of meanness.
- **Labrador Retriever** — Same energy. Will protect you, love you, never let you down.
- **German Shepherd** — The Paladin who went into law enforcement. Loyal to the end.
- **Great Pyrenees** — Silent guardian. Will stand in the cold all night protecting the flock.
- **Bernese Mountain Dog** — Gentle giant. Paladin with soft energy.

## Barbarian — Wild, Chaotic, Unstoppable

High energy, low impulse control, maximum enthusiasm. These breeds own it.

- **Husky** — The Barbarian who also won't stop talking. Chaotic good.
- **Jack Russell Terrier** — Pound for pound the most Barbarian dog alive.
- **Boxer** — Pure love, expressed entirely through physical force.
- **Weimaraner** — Beautiful chaos. Needs a job or they will make one.
- **Dalmatian** — Gorgeous Barbarian with stamina that doesn't quit.

## Wizard — Intelligent, Calculating, Independent

The smart ones. The ones who have already figured out what you're going to do before you do it.

- **Border Collie** — The Archmage of the dog world. Disturbingly intelligent.
- **Poodle** — Second most intelligent breed. Also stylish, which Wizards respect.
- **Australian Shepherd** — High-energy Wizard. Always solving something.
- **Doberman Pinscher** — Wizard who also happens to be extremely intimidating.
- **Belgian Malinois** — Combat Wizard. Terrifyingly capable.

## Ranger — Patient, Observant, Self-Reliant

Independent, calm, and deeply aware of their environment.

- **Vizsla** — The quintessential Ranger. Athletic, gentle, deeply attentive.
- **Brittany Spaniel** — Born to range. Tireless, focused, self-sufficient.
- **Rhodesian Ridgeback** — Ranger who has faced lions and remained calm.
- **Basenji** — Ancient, quiet, deeply observant. Barely barks. Watching everything.
- **Irish Setter** — Beautiful Ranger with boundless energy in the field.

## Rogue — Quiet, Cunning, Mysteriously Well-Behaved

They move without sound. They know things. You're not sure how.

- **Shiba Inu** — The Rogue's Rogue. Independent, cunning, does not care about your rules.
- **Whippet** — Moves like smoke. Quiet, fast, gone before you noticed.
- **Pharaoh Hound** — Ancient and mysterious. Clearly knows something you don't.
- **Chihuahua** — Small Rogue with enormous confidence and zero fear.
- **Greyhound** — Retired Rogue. Silent, fast, unbothered.

## Cleric — Gentle, Healing, Emotionally Attuned

The empaths. They know when you're sad. They come to you.

- **Cavalier King Charles Spaniel** — Born Cleric. Gentle, devoted, lives to comfort.
- **Saint Bernard** — The healing class in physical form. Enormous, gentle, rescues people.
- **Newfoundland** — Nanny dog. Calm, patient, watches over everyone.
- **Havanese** — Small Cleric. Incredibly attuned to human emotion.
- **Bichon Frise** — Cheerful, gentle, effortlessly healing.

## Druid — Earthy, Intuitive, Ancient

Deepest connection to nature and instinct. Most alive outdoors.

- **Bloodhound** — Ancient instinct made flesh. Follows the world through scent.
- **Basset Hound** — Slow, ancient, deeply connected to something you can't smell.
- **Irish Wolfhound** — Old soul energy. Wise, patient, connected to something primal.
- **Alaskan Malamute** — Wolf energy, not Husky chaos. Deeper, older, more Druid.
- **Chow Chow** — Ancient breed, solitary, operates on its own spiritual frequency.

## Bard — Social, Magnetic, Irresistible

The dog everyone wants to pet. The dog who performs. The dog who has *it*.

- **Corgi** — Born performer. Knows exactly how cute they are. Uses it constantly.
- **Beagle** — Friendly, vocal, impossible to ignore.
- **Pomeranian** — Tiny Bard with the energy of a stadium show.
- **French Bulldog** — Urban Bard. Maximum charm, minimum effort.
- **Pembroke Welsh Corgi** — Already royalty. Already knows it.
